SUMMARY DOC: AI-Integrated Ad Management Platform (AI-AMP)

For Holtzbrinck Publishing Group

The Problem

Across our diverse imprints, marketing and editorial teams are consistently stretched thin.

They often face three critical challenges when it comes to digital advertising:

  • Limited personnel to manage ad creation and optimization.
  • Lack of in-house expertise in digital ad strategy across platforms.
  • Insufficient time to monitor performance and adjust campaigns dynamically.

As a result, campaigns are often underperforming, inefficient, or outsourced to third parties who lack deep alignment with our brands and long-term goals.

The Current Reality

  • Teams are forced to rely on fragmented solutions and external agencies.
  • External vendors lack a stake in our success, leading to missed opportunities.
  • Audience data is often either completely untracked or mismanaged/utilized incorrectly
  • We lose agility and control, and often end up overpaying for mediocre results.

The Opportunity: One Unified Platform

We propose the development of a centralized ad-creation and management platform with seamless AI integration.

This tool will empower teams across Holtzbrinck imprints to independently launch, optimize, and report on campaigns without needing deep advertising expertise.

Key Features

✅ Multi-Channel Integration

Launch and manage campaigns across Meta, Google, TikTok, and Amazon — all from one interface by interfacing with the respective platforms’ APIs.

✅ AI-Driven Optimization

Live campaign analysis and automatic adjustment suggestions based on real-time performance metrics.

✅ Smart Budget Management

Automated recommendations on how to reallocate spend across platforms for best ROI.

✅ Audience Management Tools

Easily build, segment, and reuse high-performing audience sets across campaigns and platforms — with AI suggestions based on title, genre, behavior, and performance trends.

✅ Post-Campaign Reporting

Instant, digestible performance summaries with clear insights for editorial and marketing teams.

✅ User-Friendly Workflow

Built with busy teams in mind: intuitive UI, smart defaults, and automation-first design.

Strategic Impact

  • Reduce dependence on external agencies.
  • Elevate campaign performance through real-time AI insights.
  • Enable smaller imprints and teams to run effective campaigns with minimal overhead.
  • Build a repeatable, scalable model for performance marketing across the entire Holtzbrinck ecosystem.
