SLM Performance: Punching Above Their Weight

Modern SLMs are proving that capability isn't just about parameter count. Through advanced architectures and training, models with under 10 billion parameters are matching or even exceeding the performance of LLMs that are 10-20 times larger on specific, agent-critical tasks like tool calling and reasoning.

Illustrative comparison of SLM vs. LLM performance on specialized agentic tasks. SLMs show comparable or superior results with a fraction of the parameters.
